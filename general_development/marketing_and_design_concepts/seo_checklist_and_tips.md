### SEO checklist
- Responsiveness
- Usability test
- Visual consistence
- Contrast test
- Page Structure well defined based on a solid site wireframe
- Copy content must be focused on the client, not in the business
- Programatic SEO configured (sitemap mapping multiple pages)
- Text length balanced, not too much for a beautiful design however not too less for a good SEO.
- Solid page title (max 60 characters) and page description (max 120 characters). Both data will be displayed on Google.
- Heading elements well structured (check on SEO META in 1 Click extension)
- Two clicks rule attended: user gets what he wants only onto two clicks?
- The title on hero section (H1) must contains keywords used in the search
- Repeat keywords cross the site at least 5 times
- Use real photos and certificates cross the site
- Add alt atribute into the all website images
- Have a single H1 on the whole page. On the hero section
- Provide title and description for each page
- Provide a solid and inuitive OG image for the site be correctly shared cross social net.

### Monitoring your site on Google
1. Define the words related to your site and register it on SERanking.com.
2. Follow up the ranking on SERanking.com.

### General tips
- Each word has a cost on Google to be published and find in the search.
- The website copy, structure, and aesthetic impacts directly on the site SEO.
- The internet user prefers the organic link instead of the paid one because the paid one can be a scam.
- SEO improvements need at least 10 days to generated some solid result (increase on Google ranking).
- Google shows variated results when the search is not specific. Example: "Dentista" can return dentist ads, dentist articles, news associated to dentists, and so on.
- Think and create blog articles containing words with high demand on Google search on it's title.
- Do not create articles with even words or similar titles because it will generate conflicts on Google and Google can hide it from results.
- The articles can not be repeated or conflict between themselves. Analise it manually, remove the duplicated and merge articles if possible.
- Google increases the site ranking if it has a solid blog.
- Sites with 5 pages has more chances to be better positioned than one page websites because it's content is larger.
- Backlinks are links in others sites pointing to your site.
- When Instagram profile is ranked before your site, it means your site has a not good SEO.
- Domains with extension .gov are stronger than regular domains and have priority on engine search.
- Avoid using videos on hero section. Its beautiful, however it reduces page speed initial loading. If removing the video is not an option, reduces its quality and compress it to file be smaller.
- SEO is a life term work. Do not offer SEO work for one payment because in the future the client will say he is not obtain results.
