# SEO Checklist and Tips

## Eliminatory Criteria (Rebuild the site if any applies)

Before investing in SEO, evaluate whether the site is worth optimizing or needs to be rebuilt.

- **Single page / LP site** — no internal pages means no institutional SEO is possible. Rebuild with full architecture (home, services, about, contact, blog).
- **Not responsive (doesn't adapt to mobile)** — Google uses mobile-first indexing. 70%+ of Brazilian traffic comes from mobile. Without responsiveness the site dies on Google.

## Evaluation Tools

- **PageSpeed Insights** (pagespeed.web.dev) — overall site speed score
- **GTmetrix** (gtmetrix.com) — real load time; ideal under 1 second
- **SEO Meta in 1 Click** (Chrome extension) — heading hierarchy visualization
- **Wappalyzer** (Chrome extension) — detects what CMS/platform the site uses
- **Screaming Frog / Broken Link Checker** — finds broken links and orphan pages
- **DevTools (F12) or browser resize** — quick responsiveness check

## SEO Checklist

### Visual and Competition

- Compare with the top 3–5 competitors on Google's 1st page for the main keyword — does the site convey the same professionalism and modernity? An outdated layout drives visitors away and hurts conversion.
- Visual consistency: typography (same fonts, clear hierarchy), color palette (consistent use), uniform alignment and spacing.
- Responsiveness / mobile adaptation:
  - Clickable phone number (tap-to-call) visible in the 1st fold
  - WhatsApp icon visible and clickable (preferably floating)
  - Main CTA button large enough for a thumb
  - Hamburger menu functional and shows only essentials
  - Text readable without zoom (minimum equivalent to 16px)
  - 1st fold shows useful content, not just a banner covering the screen
  - Images scale correctly — no cropping, no distortion
- Contrast test

### Structure and Information Sequence

- Page structure well defined based on a solid site wireframe
- Logical content hierarchy: services first → about the company → testimonials → accessible blog → footer with complete NAP (name, address, phone)
- Copy content must be focused on the client, not in the business
- CTAs (contact/WhatsApp/scheduling) visible in the 1st fold AND at strategic points after services and testimonials
- Two clicks rule: user gets what they want in up to 2 clicks + some scrolling
- Provide a solid and intuitive OG image for the site to be correctly shared across social networks

### Content Quality and Relevance

- The main keyword must appear in: title tag, meta description, H1, and throughout the main text body — without this, Google doesn't understand what the page is about
- The title on hero section (H1) must contain keywords used in the search
- Have a single H1 on the whole page, on the hero section
- Heading elements well structured: H1 unique, H2 for main sections, H3 for subsections (check with SEO META in 1 Click extension)
- Solid page title (max 60 characters) and page description (max 120–160 characters) — both displayed on Google
- Repeat keywords across the site at least 5 times
- Text length balanced: not too long for a beautiful design, not too short for good SEO
- Provide title and description for each page
- Programmatic SEO configured (sitemap mapping multiple pages)
- Credibility signals: real testimonials with name and photo, real team, numbers and credentials (years in market, certifications)
- Use real photos and certificates across the site
- Add `alt` attribute to all website images

### Platform and Scalability (CMS)

- Site is on WordPress or another CMS that allows editing title, meta tags, headings, and content without depending on a developer (use Wappalyzer to detect)
- Site speed: ideally loads in under 1 second — evaluate with PageSpeed Insights and GTmetrix, compare against best competitor in the segment
- Scalable structure: can add service pages, blog, and landing pages without redoing the architecture

## Pre-SEO Technical Checks

These items are not layout/design issues but must be verified before starting SEO work:

- **Broken links** — use Screaming Frog or Broken Link Checker
- **Orphan pages** — pages with no internal links pointing to them
- **404 errors** — in main URLs
- **Indexation** — verify in Google Search Console
- **Robots.txt and Sitemap.xml** — configured correctly (see explanation below)


## Service Page Checklist

Apply when the site offers services and each service has a dedicated page:

- Dedicated page per service (not all services crammed into one page)
- Page title aligned with the search term — e.g., "Dental Implant in Balneário Camboriú" instead of just "Implants"
- Page description: addresses the client's pain + explains how it is solved
- Proof of execution: real case, client testimonial, before/after photos
- Service-specific CTA — clear button like "Schedule Implant Consultation" instead of a generic "Contact Us"

## Monitoring your site on Google
1. Define the words related to your site and register it on SERanking.com.
2. Follow up the ranking on SERanking.com.

### General tips
- Each word has a cost on Google to be published and find in the search.
- The website copy, structure, and aesthetic impacts directly on the site SEO.
- The internet user prefers the organic link instead of the paid one because the paid one can be a scam.
- SEO improvements need at least 10 days to generated some solid result (increase on Google ranking).
- Google shows variated results when the search is not specific. Example: "Dentista" can return dentist ads, dentist articles, news associated to dentists, and so on.
- Think and create blog articles containing words with high demand on Google search on it's title.
- Do not create articles with even words or similar titles because it will generate conflicts on Google and Google can hide it from results.
- The articles can not be repeated or conflict between themselves. Analise it manually, remove the duplicated and merge articles if possible.
- Google increases the site ranking if it has a solid blog.
- Sites with 5+ pages have more chances of better positioning than one-page websites because they have larger content.
- Backlinks are links in other sites pointing to yours — they improve authority.
- When an Instagram profile ranks before your site, your site has poor SEO.
- Domains with `.gov` extension are stronger than regular domains and have priority in search engines.
- Avoid using videos on the hero section — they reduce initial page load speed. If unavoidable, compress the video to a smaller file.
- Structure content using headings effectively: `h1` for the most important content, down to `h6` for the least important.
- Craft a compelling meta description (50–160 characters) as it appears in Google search results.
- SEO is a long-term ongoing work.
- The better the UX on your site, the more traffic you can receive and the more chances the user will buy your product.
- Content marketing (blog posts, eBooks, videos) combined with SEO is the most effective organic traffic strategy.
