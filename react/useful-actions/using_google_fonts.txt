Using Google Fonts in your React Porject

1)Goes to https://fonts.google.com, and click in the font choiced.
2)Choice the style of the font and click 'Select this style'.
3)In the tab Embed, click in @import, copy the cody and paste in your css file.
4)Set the font-family to you new Google font. Example:
 font-family: 'Comic Neue', cursive;
5)Import your css in App.js.
