===============================JAMSATCK====================================

JamStack means Javscript API Markup and is a concept where is possible cre-
ate funtionalities in the front-end without a back-end.


CMS: Content Management System, example: WordPress.
Headless CMS: Are CMS without the editor panel having only the panel for 
manage API, examples: Strapi, Ghost, Prismic and Shopfy.
